# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 07, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Vivd Labs** ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our mobile applications.

## 1. Personal Information Collection
We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location) from our users. 

## 2. Third-Party Services
While we do not collect your data directly, our apps may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you. This is primarily for the purpose of serving advertisements and analyzing app performance.

These third-party providers may include:
* **Google Play Services**
* **AdMob** (Google’s advertising platform)

These providers may collect technical data such as your device's Advertising ID, IP address, and basic device information. We encourage you to review their privacy policies on their respective websites.

## 3. Children’s Privacy
Our applications do not knowingly collect information from children. Since we do not collect any personal information, we are in compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## 4. Security
We value your trust. Even though we do not collect personal data, we ensure that our app builds follow industry-standard security practices to keep your device safe.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
